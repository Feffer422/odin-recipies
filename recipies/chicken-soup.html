Chicken Soup

Odin Recipies

Description

A basic yet wholesome chicken soup that will leave everyone feeling satisfied; including the cook, with easy to incorporate substitutions.

Ingredients

  • 1 raw chicken
  • 4 big carrots chopped and halved
  • 2 zuccini chopped and quartered
  • 2 onions halved and sliced
  • 2&1/2 cups of frozen peas
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • tumeric until soup becomes colored
